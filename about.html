About me

I'm a passionate artist who finds joy in painting and the intricate world of coding during my free time. With a paintbrush in one hand and a keyboard in the other, I seamlessly blend creativity and logic to express myself both on canvas and in the digital realm. My artwork is a visual journey through a kaleidoscope of emotions, reflecting the beauty I see in the world around me. What sets me apart is my ability to connect with everyone. Speaking of travel, exploration fuels my creative spirit. I believe that every journey unlocks new perspectives, influencing both my art and my understanding of the world. As a proud member of the NCC (National Cadet Corps), I bring discipline and dedication to everything I do. Whether I'm creating art, writing code, or engaging in conversations, the values instilled in me through NCC enhance my commitment to excellence.
